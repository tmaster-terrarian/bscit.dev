# Nanna's Granola
dead simple and tasty.

yields 1 gallon

contains: coconut

## oven
preheat oven to 350&deg;F

## ingredients
- 1 cup sesame seeds
- 1 cup sunflower seeds
- 2 cups coconut shavings
- 2/3 cup oil
- 8 cups oatmeal
- 1/3 cup honey
- 1/3 cup molasses
- 2 cups apple chunks (optional)

## process
In large skillet over medium heat, dry toast the sesame seeds. Put in large bowl. Dry toast sunflower seeds, add to bowl. Dry toast coconut, add to bowl. Using half the oats and half the oil at the same time, toast the oats. Toast both halves and add to bowl. Mix the honey and the molasses together. Put half the oat mixture and half the honey mixture back in the skillet and heat through. Spead on cookie sheet. Repeat with other half. Bake in oven at 350&deg;F for 10-15 minutes or until golden brown. Remove from pan. Add apples if desired or raisins, craisins, nuts or anything else that sounds good. If you add nuts then dry toast them before adding. Makes one gallon.
