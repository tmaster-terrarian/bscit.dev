# Banana Bread
delicious and soft, moist bread.

yields 1-2 loaves

contains: gluten, dairy, eggs

## oven
preheat oven to 350&deg;F

## ingredients
dry
- 1 3/4 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt

wet
- 1 tbsp butter
- 1/3 cup vegetable shortening
- 2/3 cup sugar
- 2 eggs, well beaten
- 3 very ripe bananas (more than 30% black), mashed

## process
1. whisk dry ingredients together in a small bowl.
2. in a separate bowl, cream shortening and butter. add beaten eggss to shortening mixture, beating until light and fluffy.
3. alternate between adding dry ingredients and banana mash, mixing well after every addition.
4. pour into greased loaf pans and bake at 350&deg;F for 40 to 50 minutes, until top is dry.
5. let stand in pan for 10 minutes before removing or cooling.
