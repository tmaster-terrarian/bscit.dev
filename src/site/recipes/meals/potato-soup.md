# Potato Soup
creamy starchy goodness.

yields one big pot of soup

contains: dairy

## ingredients
- 10 potatoes, peeled, diced 3/4", boiled
- 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
soup base
- 58 oz chicken stock
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 2 cups water
roux
- 1 cup butter
- 3/4 cup flour

## process
1. combine butter and flour into a roux over low-med heat, boil for 2 minutes.
2. in a pot, bring potatoes, chicken stock, onion, salt, pepper, and water to boil
3. as soon as the big pot is boiling, add roux, boil for 15 minutes
4. add heavy cream to the pot, boil for 5 minutes
5. top with bacon, cheese, chives, or anything else interesting
