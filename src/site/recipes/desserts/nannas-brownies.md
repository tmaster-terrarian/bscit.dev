# Nanna's Brownies
excellent cream and chocolate dish.

yields 9x11x2" of brownies

contains: gluten, dairy, eggs

## oven
preheat oven to 350&deg;F

## ingredients
chocolate
- 4 tbsp vegetable shortening
- 2/4 cup melted butter
- 3/4 cup cocoa

wet
- 4 eggs
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 tsp vanilla

dry
- 1 1/3 cup flour
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp baking powder

icing and glaze (optional)
- butter cream frosting
- 1 1/2 tsp mint extract
- cooled chocolate glaze

## process
1. in sauce pan over low heat, combine shortening, melted butter, and cocoa together. stir continually until melted, then cover and set aside.
2. combine dry ingredients in a bowl and set aside.
3. in a separate bowl, beat eggs and gradually add sugar. mix well.
4. mix in chocolate and vanilla.
5. add dry mixture and mix well.
6. pour into a 9x11" baking pan and bake at 350&deg;F for 25 minutes. DO NOT overback. the edges will look dry byt the center will look under-cooked.
7. let it cool completely.

optional icing and glaze:
1. mix mint extract into butter cream frosting, frost the top of the brownies
2. let stand for 20 minutes
3. pour cooled chocolate glaze over the top, then let stand until solid.
