# Magic Cookie Bars
these cookie bars come in two layers, one is crunchy and the other is nutty and creamy

yields 1 tray of cookies

contains: gluten, nuts, dairy, coconut

## oven
preheat oven to 350&deg;F

## ingredients
crust
- 1 package graham crackers, crushed
- 1/2 cup butter, melted
- 3 tbsp sugar

topping
- 1 cup walnuts
- 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips
- 1 1/3 cups coconut shavings
- 14oz (1 can) sweetened condensed milk

## process
- in a bowl, mix together the crushed graham crackers, butter, and sugar
- spread the mixture evenly across your cookie tray
- cover the layer evenly with walnuts, choc chips, and coconut
- drizzle the sweetened condensed milk over the top
- bake at 350&deg;F for 20-25min
- let stand for a minute or two, then cut into squares or rectangles

## bscit's notes
- if you add the condensed milk after baking, it drastically changes the end result. depends on what you're into!
