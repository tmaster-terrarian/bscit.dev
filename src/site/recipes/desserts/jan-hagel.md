# Jan Hagel
incredibly tasty buttery holiday cookies.

contains: gluten, nuts, eggs, dairy

## oven
preheat oven to 300&deg;F

## ingredients
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 cups flour
- 0.5 tsp cinnamon

topping
- Chopped nut meats

## process
let butter stand until soft. Cream with sugar. Mix in egg yolk. Sift flour with cinnamon and add a little at a time. knead a little. Pat down in Thin Cookie Sheet. Work it out with fingers. Brush with slightly beaten egg white to which has been added a little water. Sprinkle with chopped nuts. Bake at 300&deg;F for 30 minutes.

## bscit's notes
- NOT SHEET PAN!
- making this one egg-free proves to be a challenge
