# Key Lime Pie
probably one of my favorite pastries.

yields 1 entire pie

contains: gluten, dairy

## oven
preheat oven to 325&deg;F

## ingredients
filling
- 28oz (2 cans) sweetened condensed milk
- 3/4 cup + 2 tbsp key lime juice

crust
- 1 pkg graham crackers, crushed
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp nutmeg, ground
- 1/4 lb butter, melted

## process
- in a skillet on low-med heat, mix condensed milk and key lime for 15 minutes.
- in a bowl, mix together the crushed graham crackers, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
- add melted butter to the bowl.
- press crust mix into an even layer in a spring form pan. add filling, cover w/ parchment paper + tape. bake for 20 minutes at 325&deg;F.
