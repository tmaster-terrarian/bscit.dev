# Buttermilk Donuts
simple plain flat donuts.

contains: gluten, dairy, eggs

## fryer/skillet
heat oil to 365&deg;F

## ingredients
dry
- 2 1/4 cups sifted flour
- 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp nutmeg

wet
- 1/2 cup buttermilk, room temperature
- 2 tbsp melted butter
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- 1 large beaten egg, room temperature
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar

## process
1. combine dry ingredients in a separate bowl from combined wet ingredients.
2. add dry to wet, beat until dough unsticks from sides of the bowl.
3. turn out onto well floured board, knead for 30 seconds.
4. pat and roll to 1/3" thickness, let stand for 1 minute.
5. dip cutter in flour before each cut.
6. lower disc into hot oil, with a spatula to prevent breaking.
7. fry on each side until golden brown.

## recipe notes
- optional last step: coat in powdered sugar or cinnamon sugar to make it sweeter.

## bscit's notes
- the oil can get very scary! if you use a skillet, try to use one with high walls.
