# Chicken Gravy
yields ~3 cups

contains: meat

## ingredients
- 2 tbsp butter
- 2 tbsp flour
- 2 cups chicken broth
- 6-8 grinds salt
- 10 grinds pepper
- 3-4 shakes McCormick's Chicken Seasoning
- 1 shake McCormick's Steak Seasoning

## process
- roux the butter and flour
- add chicken broth to roux, bring to boil
- add seasonings
- boil for 7-10 minutes until thickened

## bscit's notes
- i don't think i've ever found an alternative to McCormick's seasonings. if you know any, please shoot me an email!
