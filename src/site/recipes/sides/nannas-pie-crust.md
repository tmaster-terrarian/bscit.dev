# Nanna's Pie Crust
yields about enough for 2 pies

contains: gluten

## oven
preheat oven to 425&deg;F

## ingredients
- 2 cups flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 3/4 cup vegetable shortening
- ~1/3 cup cold water

## process
- repeat the following steps twice using half the ingredients each time
- mix together flour and salt
- cut(?) in shortening
- sprinkle with water and toss with fork
- moist, not gooey!
- cling into ball, gently push together
- plastic wrap on counter, pinch and sprinkle flour
- plop dough on top, flip over, add a little flour
- plastic wrap on top, roll to side
- if baking: remove plastic wrap and bake in pie tray at 425&deg;F for ~30 minutes
