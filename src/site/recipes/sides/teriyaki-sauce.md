# Teriyaki Sauce
contains: soy, corn

## ingredients
part 1
- 0.75 cup soy sauce
- 0.5 cup water
- 0.25 cup brown sugar
- 0.25 tsp ground ginger

part 2
- 0.25 cup water
- 1.5 tbsp corn starch

## process
1. bring the first 0.5 cup of water with the soy sauce, brown sugar, and ground ginger to boil.
2. in a separate bowl, mix together 0.25 cup of water and corn starch, then add to the pot.
3. simmer for 1-2 minutes.
